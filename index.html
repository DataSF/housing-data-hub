Welcome to the Housing Data Hub! This is your go-to resource for learning about policies and programs that affect housing affordability and are administered by local government agencies in San Francisco. We provide an overview of each of the major programs - what it is, its purpose, and related data.

Who is this for? This is for policy makers, decision makers and individuals interested in what San Francisco is doing about affordable housing. If you need affordable housing or want to access these programs, please visit the Mayor's Office of Housing and Community Development.
